What is this website about?

Our website allows users to live in comfort by preventing small problems within the household.
For example, users might forget to turn off the lights, wanting to cool off after work or even
wanting to get some household chores done while away. With this website, you will be able to turn
off the lights via wireless network and even turn on the air conditioner while you are at work!
